Questions About Our Farm?

Do you offer farm tours?
Currently, our farm operates in a highly-sterilized environment, so public tours are restricted to maintain purity. We do offer virtual tours during our Masterclasses!
Where do you source your spores?
Most of our proprietary strains are generated in-house through selective breeding by our mycologists over the last decade.
Can I pause or cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can pause or cancel anytime through your dashboard. No long-term commitments required.
What comes in the standard box?
Each box contains 3-4 varieties of fresh, seasonally available mushrooms, a recipes guide, and a small surprise like a dried herb or seasoning.
Do you ship nationwide?
Currently, we ship to all contiguous 48 states to ensure mushroom freshness upon delivery.
