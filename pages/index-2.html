Shop FAQ

When will my kit ship?
All kits ship within 24 hours of ordering.
How long does a kit take to fruit?
Most of our kits will begin to fruit within 7-14 days, depending on the variety and your indoor environment.
Do I need special equipment for the grow kits?
No! Everything you need is included in the box. You just need a spray bottle (provided) and a little bit of water.
How many harvests can I get from one kit?
You can usually get 2-3 harvests (flushes) from a single kit before the substrate is exhausted.
